A long long time ago lived an old women called Prushka. She had a hut in the middle of a dense, creepy forest, and an excessive amount of candlelight. She was a humble women, and a lonely women. Each day her lonliness grew and grew, slowly filling up her heart. As her lonliness grew, she became paranoid about the world around her. The forest seemed like the dark unkown, and her hut had stopped being her home. After years and years, there was a knock at her door. A sharp, menacing, knock.
